Care & Cleaning Guide — Spacioworks

Proper maintenance helps your furniture and interiors look beautiful and perform flawlessly for years.

A. General Care

  1. Clean surfaces regularly with a soft, dry, or slightly damp cloth.
  2. Avoid strong chemicals, detergents, or abrasive cleaning pads.
  3. Wipe spills immediately; do not let moisture stand on surfaces.
  4. Avoid direct exposure to sunlight, heat, or humidity for long periods.
  5. Keep interiors well-ventilated to prevent mold and odor.

B. Cabinets and Shutters

  1. Do not overload shelves or drawers beyond rated capacity.
  2. Use handles or knobs; avoid pulling from edges.
  3. Do not lean, climb, or sit on cabinetry or countertops.

C. Hardware and Accessories

  1. Use gently; close doors and drawers softly.
  2. If movement becomes stiff, contact Spacioworks for adjustment.
  3. Avoid spraying perfumes, fresheners, or acids near metallic parts.

D. Stone, Quartz, or Solid Surfaces

  1. Clean with mild soap and water; wipe dry.
  2. Avoid placing hot utensils directly; use trivets.
  3. Do not cut directly; use a chopping board.

E. Glass and Mirrors

  1. Use a clean, lint-free cloth with mild glass cleaner.
  2. Avoid hard wiping or scratching.

F. Soft Furnishings and Upholstery

  1. Vacuum regularly with a soft brush.
  2. Keep away from direct sunlight to prevent fading.
